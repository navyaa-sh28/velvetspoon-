About Us

Welcome to Velvet Spoon – where healthy, eco-friendly vegetarian delights meet convenience and love for the planet!

  • Fasting-friendly food: Special dishes crafted for fasting and dietary needs without compromising on taste.
  • Organic ingredients: Every meal is prepared using fresh, organic, and sustainably sourced ingredients.
  • Fast Delivery: Get your favorite meals delivered swiftly via Swiggy, Zomato, or directly through our website.
  • Mini Sustainable Packaging: Meals come in eco-friendly packaging to reduce waste while keeping food fresh and delicious.
  • Wholesome Experience: Dining with us is about health, sustainability, and a delightful culinary journey all on your plate.

At Velvet Spoon, we believe in serving food that nourishes your body, delights your taste buds, and respects our planet.
